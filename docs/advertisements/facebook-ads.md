# Facebook Ad Copy & Campaigns

Facebook is the primary digital platform for Filipino sari-sari store owners. This file outlines direct response Facebook Ad campaigns designed to drive app installs.

---

## Campaign 1: "Goodbye, Utang Notebook!"

- **Objective:** App Installs / Leads
- **Targeting:** Interests in Micro-business, Retail, Sari-sari stores, aged 25-55 in the Philippines.
- **Pain Point:** Forgotten credits, lost notebooks, hours spent calculating balances.

### Creative Option A (Video/Reels Ad)

- **Visual:** Ate Sarah holding an old, damp, dirty notebook with pages falling out. She looks frustrated. Then she holds up her clean, modern phone showing the **SariSari** app.
- **Hook (First 3 seconds):** _"Nawawala ba ang utang notebook mo? O baka naman hindi na mabasa ang nakasulat?"_
- **Primary Text:**
  > ❌ Iwasan ang sunog-bato sa utang! Ang lumang notebook, madaling mabasa, mawala, o mapunit.
  >
  > Gamitin ang **SariSari App**—ang digital ledger na kasya sa iyong bulsa.
  > ✅ I-record ang utang at bayad ng suki sa isang tap lang.
  > ✅ Kusa nitong kinukuwenta ang natitirang balance gamit ang FIFO calculation.
  > ✅ 100% offline at libre! Walang kailangang load o signal.
  >
  > I-click ang download button sa ibaba para i-upgrade ang iyong tindahan ngayon! 👇
- **Headline:** 📓 Paalam Notebook, Hello Digital Ledger!
- **Call to Action:** Install Now

### Creative Option B (Image/Carousel Ad)

- **Visual:** Side-by-side comparison.
  - Left: Notebook with crossed-out text, ink stains, and a calculator. Label: "Magulo at Nakakalito"
  - Right: High-contrast screenshot of SariSari's Suki balance page showing clear ledger lines. Label: "Malinaw at Sigurado"
- **Headline:** Sigurado ang Listahan sa SariSari
- **Call to Action:** Download

## Campaign 2: "Brownout & No-Signal Proof POS"

- **Objective:** App Installs
- **Targeting:** Rural areas, regions with frequent power outages, small business owners.
- **Pain Point:** Apps that freeze or require active internet connections to check stock or log a sale.

### Creative Option A (Single Image Ad)

- **Visual:** Ate Sarah smiling in her store. There is a lightning/rain icon indicating bad weather outside. She is happily scanning/selecting items on her phone screen, which has a prominent offline badge: "100% Offline-First".
- **Primary Text:**
  > 🌧️ Bagyo? Brownout? Walang signal sa inyong barangay?
  >
  > Huwag patigilin ang takbo ng iyong sari-sari store! Ang **SariSari App** ay ginawa para sa totoong kondisyon sa Pilipinas.
  >
  > 📶 **Zero Data Required:** Ang lahat ng iyong produkto, benta, at utang ay ligtas na naka-save sa loob mismo ng iyong phone. Hindi kailangan ng internet para mag-compute o mag-imbentaryo.
  >
  > subukan ang pinakamaaasahang offline-first app para sa mga tindera!
- **Headline:** ⚡ Kahit Walang Internet, Tuloy ang Benta!
- **Call to Action:** Download Now

## Campaign 3: "Tindahan Upgrade (Modernization)"

- **Objective:** Brand Awareness & App Installs
- **Targeting:** Tech-forward tinderas/tinderos, users of digital wallets (GCash, Maya).
- **Pain Point:** Feeling like their business is stagnant or hard to audit.

### Creative Option A (Video Ad)

- **Visual:** Fast transitions of products being scanned/tapped in the app, the profit reports showing up in graphs, and a clean interface. Very modern, upbeat background music.
- **Primary Text:**
  > 🚀 Gawing high-tech ang iyong sari-sari store ngayong 2026!
  >
  > Hindi lang pang-malalaking grocery ang POS at inventory system. Ngayon, kasya na ito sa Android or iOS phone mo gamit ang **SariSari**.
  >
  > 🌟 **Features na Magpapadali ng Buhay Tindahan:**
  > • Fast POS: Auto-compute ng sukli para sa mabilis na transaction.
  > • Inventory Alerts: Malalaman mo agad kung ano ang low stock para maihanda sa supplier.
  > • Kita Reports: Kita mo agad ang iyong araw-araw na tubo nang walang kalkulador.
  >
  > Libreng gamitin. Walang hidden fees. 100% Offline.
- **Headline:** 📱 I-upgrade ang Tindahan Mo Ngayon!
- **Call to Action:** Learn More / Install
