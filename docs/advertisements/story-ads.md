# Vertical Story Ads Scripts (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)

Vertical short-form video (9:16) is the most engaging medium for reaching our core audience in the Philippines. This document details complete script drafts designed for Facebook Reels, Instagram Stories, and TikTok.

---

## Script 1: "The Lost Notebook Drama" (Humorous / Relatable)

- **Format:** 9:16 Video, 30 Seconds
- **Tone:** Funny, dramatic (reminiscent of Filipino soap operas)
- **Visuals:** High-contrast colors, close-up shots of expressive faces.

### Script 1 Scene-by-Scene

1. **0:00 - 0:05 (Hook):**
   - _Visual:_ Dramatic zoom-in on Ate Sarah’s shocked face. Dramatic sound effect.
   - _Ate Sarah (screaming dramatically):_ "Nasaan na? Nasaan ang listahan?!"
   - _Text on Screen:_ ⚠️ ANG NAWAWALANG NOTEBOOK (The Lost Notebook)
2. **0:05 - 0:12 (The Search):**
   - _Visual:_ Ate Sarah turns her store upside down—looking under egg crates, inside instant noodle boxes, shaking a bag of sugar.
   - _Voiceover (dramatic/deep):_ "Isang taon ng utang... nalimutan na? Paano na ang tubo ng tindahan?"
3. **0:12 - 0:20 (The Solution):**
   - _Visual:_ Kuya Jun enters, taps his phone, and shows Ate Sarah his screen.
   - _Kuya Jun:_ "Ate, kalma. I-SariSari app mo na yan. Digital, ligtas, at offline! Hindi nawawala."
4. **0:20 - 0:30 (Feature Demo & CTA):**
   - _Visual:_ Fast screen recording of adding an utang entry in the app. Ends with Ate Sarah smiling, holding her phone.
   - _Voiceover:_ "I-download ang SariSari App nang libre. Paalam sa nawawalang notebook! Works 100% offline."
   - _Text on Screen:_ Download Free on Android & iOS.

## Script 2: "The 3-Second Checkout Challenge" (Product Demo)

- **Format:** 9:16 Video, 15 Seconds
- **Tone:** Fast-paced, energetic, satisfying
- **Visuals:** Clean split-screen or overlay of the app in action.

### Script 2 Scene-by-Scene

1. **0:00 - 0:03 (Hook):**
   - _Visual:_ A timer counting down from 3.00 seconds.
   - _Text on Screen:_ Can we checkout in 3 seconds? ⏱️
2. **0:03 - 0:07 (The Scan/Tap):**
   - _Visual:_ Owner taps "Coke 1.5L" and "Rebisco Crackers" on the SariSari POS screen. Total `₱75` instantly appears.
3. **0:07 - 0:12 (The Payment & Change):**
   - _Visual:_ Customer hands a ₱100 bill. Owner taps `₱100` on the screen. The change `₱25` flashes in bright green.
4. **0:12 - 0:15 (The Outro):**
   - _Visual:_ A customer smiling, grabbing their items. Phone displays the SariSari logo.
   - _Voiceover:_ "No manual math. Zero errors. Keep the line moving with SariSari. Download now!"

## Script 3: "Storm Season Test" (Utility Proof)

- **Format:** 9:16 Video, 30 Seconds
- **Tone:** Authentic, documentary style, reassuring
- **Visuals:** Cozy interior, rainy weather outside.

### Script 3 Scene-by-Scene

1. **0:00 - 0:07 (Hook):**
   - _Visual:_ Rain pouring down on a street. Sudden brownout—the store's overhead lights turn off. Ate Sarah lights a candle.
   - _Text on Screen:_ ⛈️ Brownout at Walang Signal?
2. **0:07 - 0:15 (The Problem):**
   - _Visual:_ Other digital business apps showing loading spinner: "No connection."
   - _Voiceover:_ "Ang ibang smart apps, hinto kapag walang data o signal. Pero ang tindahan mo, hindi pwedeng huminto."
3. **0:15 - 0:25 (The Action):**
   - _Visual:_ Ate Sarah opens SariSari app. It loads instantly. She checks Aling Nena's credit balance and logs a payment. A badge says: "100% Offline Connected."
4. **0:25 - 0:30 (Outro):**
   - _Visual:_ Ate Sarah smiles at the camera, holding a candle in one hand and her phone in the other.
   - _Voiceover:_ "SariSari is built for the real conditions. Download free today and stay business-ready!"
