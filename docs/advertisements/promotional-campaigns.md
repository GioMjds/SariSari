# App Launch & Promotional Campaigns

This document details key community-driven promotional campaigns designed to launch and grow the **SariSari** app user base across Philippine barangays.

---

## Campaign 1: "SariSari Upgrader" Community Program

- **Goal:** Drive rapid word-of-mouth adoption by leveraging tech-savvy youth and young store owners to onboard traditional store owners.
- **Concept:** Provide incentives for community ambassadors who help local _tinderas_ install the app and set up their initial product list.

### Campaign Mechanics

1. **Ambassador Kits:** Provide digital badges and downloadable "SariSari Partner" printouts for physical stores.
2. **Referral Reward:** When a new store lists their first 20 products and logs 10 sales, the referring ambassador and the store owner get recognized in local community highlights, or receive small micro-incentives (e.g., GCash rewards, mobile load).
3. **Marketing Copy:**
   - _"Tulungan si Ate at Kuya mag-upgrade! Be a SariSari Ambassador and help modernise your local neighborhood tindahan."_

---

## Campaign 2: "Tiwala sa Suki" (Trust & Transparency Campaign)

- **Goal:** Target store owners who are hesitant to move away from paper books because they fear customers won't trust a phone screen.
- **Concept:** Focus on the "transparency" feature. Educate owners on how they can screenshot and share a clean, formatted balance summary directly to customers via Messenger or SMS.

### Marketing Assets & Copy

- **Campaign Video:** A short story showing Aling Nena questioning a balance. The store owner taps "Share Statement" on SariSari, sending a beautiful, itemized breakdown to Aling Nena's phone. Aling Nena smiles: _"Ah, nakasulat nga dito yung kape na kinuha ko nung Martes. Salamat!"_
- **Slogan:** _"Resibong Malinaw, Kaibigang Panatag."_ (Clear receipts keep friends at peace.)

---

## Campaign 3: "SariSari Star Store" Showcase

- **Goal:** Build social proof by interviewing and showcasing real-life store owners who have successfully replaced paper notebooks with the app.
- **Content Structure:**
  - **Case Study posts:** "How Ate Sarah saved 4 hours a week on bookkeeping."
  - **Before vs. After statistics:**
    - Before: 15 minutes calculating daily sales / After: 0 seconds (automated dashboard).
    - Before: Lost ~₱300/month on unrecorded credits / After: 0 pesos lost.
- **Distribution:** Facebook Groups for local micro-business owners, TikTok success-story reels.
