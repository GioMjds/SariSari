# Seasonal & Holiday Campaigns

Filipino retail follows strong seasonal peaks. This document outlines promotional campaigns tailored for key seasons in the Philippines to align **SariSari** with high-volume business periods.

---

## 1. Paskong Masagana (The Christmas Rush)

- **Timeframe:** September to December (The "Ber" Months)
- **Focus:** Managing fast-paced bulk sales, holiday baskets, and tracking seasonal credit.
- **Pain Point:** High traffic leading to checkout congestion, stocking up on holiday bestsellers (queso de bola, fruit cocktail, spaghetti packs), and customers promising to pay credits after receiving their 13th-month pay.

### Paskong Masagana Campaign Hook

- **Slogan:** _"Masayang Pasko, Magaan ang Negosyo."_ (Happy Christmas, Light Business.)
- **Marketing Concept:**
  Show Ate Sarah handling a crowd of holiday shoppers with ease using the SariSari POS. While other store owners are sweating over calculations, Ate Sarah is smiling, tapping her screen, and collecting cash.
- **Key Features Promoted:** Fast checkout POS, automatic change computing, and holiday inventory tracking.

---

## 2. Balik-Eskwela (Back-to-School Season)

- **Timeframe:** May to June (or school opening months)
- **Focus:** Stocking school supplies, snacks, juices, and tracking daily allowances.
- **Pain Point:** Transitioning inventory from summer items to school supplies (pads of paper, pencils, snacks) and managing credits for parents buying school items.

### Balik-Eskwela Campaign Hook

- **Slogan:** _"Handang-Handa sa Balik-Eskwela."_ (All set for back-to-school.)
- **Marketing Concept:**
  An educational post showing how to set up a "School Supplies" category in the inventory. Emphasize low-stock notifications for items that sell out fast in the mornings (e.g., intermediate pads, blue pens, juice boxes).
- **Key Features Promoted:** Inventory categories, custom stock warnings, and credit tracking for parents.

---

## 3. Tag-Init at Fiesta (Summer & Festival Season)

- **Timeframe:** March to May
- **Focus:** Tracking fast-moving cold items (ice candy, soft drinks, beer, halo-halo ingredients).
- **Pain Point:** High demand for ice and cold drinks leading to rapid stock exhaustion; managing bulk orders for barangay fiesta feasts on credit.

### Tag-Init at Fiesta Campaign Hook

- **Slogan:** _"Malamig na Benta sa Mainit na Panahon."_ (Cool sales in hot times.)
- **Marketing Concept:**
  Focus on the speed of selling soft drinks and snacks to fiesta visitors. Showcase how the app helps track bulk orders for fiesta catering or family events, ensuring credits are documented immediately before the party starts.
- **Key Features Promoted:** Offline POS (for outdoor fiesta booths), bulk sales logging, and suki utang tracking.
