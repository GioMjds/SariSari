# App Store & Google Play Store Screenshots Plan

This document outlines the layout, visual direction, and copy for the official App Store and Google Play Store screenshots for the **SariSari** app.

---

## Visual Theme & Design Guidelines

- **Palette:** Sleek dark mode / high-contrast light mode with vibrant accent colors (deep violet, warm yellow, emerald green for successful transactions).
- **Format:** Modern device mockups (bezel-less phone) angled slightly, with bold, legible headline text above or below the mockup.
- **Backgrounds:** Minimalist gradient backdrops matching the screen's primary action.
- **Language:** Taglish/English — simple, direct, and conversational to resonate with Filipino store owners (Ate, Kuya, Tindera/Tindero).

---

## Screenshot Breakdown

### Screenshot 1: The Hero Splash

- **Headline:** **"Ang Modernong Kasama ng Iyong Tindahan"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Manage sales, inventory, and suki utang—100% offline."
- **Visual:** A high-quality rendering of the SariSari home screen/dashboard, showing quick metrics (Daily Sales, Low Stock alert indicator, Outstanding Utang).
- **Focus:** Overall brand identity, premium feel, and the promise of a modern upgrade for their business.

### Screenshot 2: Point of Sale (Sales)

- **Headline:** **"Mabilis na Benta, Kahit Busy ang Counter"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Add items, compute change, and log sales in under 3 seconds."
- **Visual:** The active POS screen displaying selected items (e.g., _Coke 1.5L_, _Lucky Me Pancit Canton_), a clear total, and a "Tinda" button ready to tap.
- **Focus:** Speed, ease of use during high-traffic hours, and automatic change calculation.

### Screenshot 3: Utang Ledger (Credits)

- **Headline:** **"Paalam, Papel na Listahan ng Utang!"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Track customer (suki) credit balances automatically. Zero errors."
- **Visual:** A suki profile detail screen (e.g., "Aling Nena") showing a clear total running balance (e.g., `₱1,250`) and a neat chronological log of credits and payments.
- **Focus:** Financial security, precision, and building trust through error-free record keeping.

### Screenshot 4: Inventory Management

- **Headline:** **"Imbentaryo na Laging Sakto"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Categorize products, track stock levels, and get low stock alerts."
- **Visual:** The inventory list screen with visual progress bars indicating stock levels, product thumbnails, and warning icons for low-stock items.
- **Focus:** Organized management, avoiding out-of-stock situations, and simple category sorting.

### Screenshot 5: Hard Offline-First

- **Headline:** **"100% Offline. Walang Internet? Walang Problema!"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Works anywhere, anytime. No signal required for core features."
- **Visual:** A screen showing a transaction being logged with an overlay badge of an airplane mode icon or a "Local Safe Database" status, illustrating that all operations execute instantly without data.
- **Focus:** Reliability, accessibility, and zero data cost.

### Screenshot 6: Reports & Insights

- **Headline:** **"Malinaw ang Kita at Takbo ng Negosyo"**
- **Sub-headline:** "Understand your daily sales, popular items, and outstanding credits."
- **Visual:** The reports screen showing simple, intuitive bar charts of weekly sales, a pie chart of top categories, and total collections.
- **Focus:** Profitability tracking, data-driven decisions, and easy tax or boundary computation.
