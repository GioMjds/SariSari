# Hero Illustration Concepts & Design Briefs

This document guides the creative direction for the primary hero illustrations used on the **SariSari** website, app onboarding flow, and promotional banners.

---

## Art Direction Guidelines

- **Style:** Modern vector illustration with soft gradients, clean lines, and friendly, expressive characters. Avoid generic stock photos; focus on authentic Filipino neighborhood aesthetics.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Dominant:** Deep violet/indigo (represents modern technology and stability).
  - **Secondary:** Warm orange/yellow accents (represents the warmth and friendliness of a local sari-sari store).
  - **Accents:** Emerald green (for positive financial growth, sales, and trust).
- **Environment:** Bright, clean, colorful sari-sari store storefronts filled with familiar items (hanging snack packs, jars of candies, glass soda bottles, egg crates) presented in a organized, modern way.

---

## Illustration Briefs

### Brief 1: "The Modernized Tindahan" (Landing Page Hero)

- **Concept:** A split or transitioning visual showing the progression of a traditional store into a digital-assisted business.
- **Composition:**
  - In the foreground: Ate Sarah (tindera) stands behind a neat, organized store counter. She holds a smartphone displaying a glowing green checkmark (successful sale).
  - On the counter: A clean, minimal arrangement of popular items (like a basket of fresh eggs, colorful juice boxes).
  - In the background: The warm, inviting interior of the store, softly lit, displaying structured shelves.
- **Mood:** Optimistic, clean, professional, yet deeply local and friendly.
- **Text Overlay (if any):** _"I-upgrade ang iyong tindahan."_

### Brief 2: "The Trust Ledger" (Onboarding Screen: Utang)

- **Concept:** Visualizing the trust and relationship between the shop owner and her suki.
- **Composition:**
  - Ate Sarah and Aling Nena (an older customer in a duster) are interacting at the counter.
  - Aling Nena is smiling as Ate Sarah holds out her phone. Between them, a light, semi-transparent digital ledger screen floats, showing a transaction ledger matching their transaction.
  - No physical notebooks are present, symbolizing the shift to digital transparency.
- **Mood:** Warm, trusting, harmonious, community-centric.
- **Focus:** Reassuring the user that digital tracking makes relationships stronger and avoids money arguments.

### Brief 3: "Offline Anywhere" (Onboarding Screen: Offline Reliability)

- **Concept:** Emphasizing the power of the local database working with zero signal.
- **Composition:**
  - Ate Sarah is recording inventory at the back of her shop, or standing in a rural area with beautiful hills in the background.
  - A subtle signal icon with a slash through it (indicating no internet) floats near her, alongside a bright green shield or battery icon showing "100% Secure & Local Database."
  - Her phone screen is bright, showing a list of products with inventory checkmarks.
- **Mood:** Reliable, stress-free, independent, powerful.
- **Focus:** Demonstrating that signal drops or storms won't interrupt her livelihood.
