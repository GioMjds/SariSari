SariSari — Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 16, 2025

Thank you for using SariSari. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Local app data: SariSari stores product, inventory, sales, and credit records locally on your device using a local database.
  • No account data: The app does not require an account, email, or password by default.
  • Optional data: If you choose to export or back up data (for example via file export), that action is initiated by you and handled by the platform's share/export flows.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data collected and stored locally is used solely to provide app functionality — managing products, tracking inventory, recording sales, and generating reports. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party libraries and services (for example analytics or UI libraries). These libraries may collect telemetry on their own terms. Please refer to each provider's privacy documentation if needed.

4. Data Storage & Security

All primary data is stored locally on your device. We recommend using your device's backup and security features. If you enable any cloud backup or export features, that data will be handled by the chosen platform or service.

5. Your Rights

You can view, edit, export, or delete your data using the app's built-in screens and export features. To remove all app data, uninstalling the app will typically delete local storage (platform-dependent).

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at your-email@example.com. Replace this email with your preferred contact address.

Note: This Privacy Policy is a basic template. If you plan to distribute the app commercially or collect personal data beyond local storage, consider consulting a legal professional and updating this policy accordingly.
