# Sari Master Prompts for Character Design

This document contains the finalized image generation prompts for Sari, the mascot of SariSari. These prompts use a hybrid approach combining a **Core DNA** (matching the exact art style of `logo.jpg`) and a **Technical Wrapper** (for game-asset quality), with specific **State Modifiers** to ensure correct emotional expression across different app screens.

## 🛠 Prompt Construction Logic

Each prompt is assembled as follows:
`[Core DNA]` + `[State Modifier]` + `[Technical Wrapper]`

**Core DNA:** A high-quality cartoon mascot sprite of a character named Sari. Sari is an anthropomorphic clear cylindrical glass jar (garapon) with thick, bold dark brown outlines. She has a flat cylindrical terracotta/brick-red lid acting as a hat. Her face features simple curved arcs for happy eyes and a cheerful open mouth showing a small tongue. Her limbs are thick white arms and legs outlined in bold dark brown. Playful cartoon sticker style, bold outlines, clean vector shapes, flat colors, centered composition.

**Technical Wrapper:** Isolated on a plain white background. Cartoon vector mascot style. Full body shot. High contrast, sharp edges. No gradients, no photorealism, no complex 3D rendering. A clean flat oval terracotta shadow on the floor beneath her.
