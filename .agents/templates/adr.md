# Architecture Decision Record (ADR)

## Metadata

- **ADR ID**: ADR-XXX
- **Title**: Short Title of Decision
- **Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Rejected | Superseded
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Authors**: Agent / Author Name

## Context

Describe the background, motivation, and problem driving this decision. Include constraints, technical requirements, and business goals.

## Decision

State the specific decision made. Explain the chosen architecture, technical stack, pattern, or strategy.

## Rationale

Explain why this solution was selected over alternative options:

- Option A: Pros and Cons
- Option B: Pros and Cons
- Selected Choice: Why it best fits our requirements

## Consequences

### Positive

- Benefit 1
- Benefit 2

### Negative / Trade-offs

- Risk or maintenance overhead 1
- Trade-off 2

## Compliance & Verification

- How will this decision be verified? (e.g. automated tests, linter rules, code review criteria)
